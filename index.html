About

Who I am and what I do

I am a software engineer with 4 years of experience building internal business systems and web applications. My work focuses on designing REST APIs, integrating existing services, and building frontends that make complex workflows intuitive. I primarily work with PHP and Vue.js on the application side, Go for high-performance services, and MySQL/PostgreSQL for data storage, while using Linux, Git, and Docker to keep deployments reliable and predictable.

I enjoy the process of building software and solving problems. To me, engineering is a bit like the Tetris board beside this section - taking individual pieces, understanding how they fit together, and arranging them to create something complete. Every system is a puzzle made up of services, data, and interfaces, and I enjoy designing solutions that bring those pieces together cleanly and efficiently.
